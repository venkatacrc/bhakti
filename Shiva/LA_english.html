Lingastakam

(English Transliteration Only)

The Eight Verses

Verse 1
Brahmamurari surarchita lingam, nirmala bhasita sobhita lingam |
Janmaja dukha vinasaka lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 1 ||
Verse 2
Devamuni pravararchita lingam, kamadahana karunakara lingam |
Ravana darpa vinashana lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 2 ||
Verse 3
Sarva sugandhi sulepita lingam, buddhi vivardhana karana lingam |
Siddha surasura vandita lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 3 ||
Verse 4
Kanaka mahamani bhushita lingam, phanipati veshtita shobhita lingam |
Daksha suyajna vinashana lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 4 ||
Verse 5
Kunkumachandra lepita lingam, pankaja hara sushobhita lingam |
Sanchita papa vinashana lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 5 ||
Verse 6
Devaganarchita sevita lingam, bhavair bhaktibhir eva cha lingam |
Dinakara koti prabhakara lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 6 ||
Verse 7
Ashtadalo pariveshtita lingam, sarva samudbhava karana lingam |
Ashtadaridra vinashana lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 7 ||
Verse 8
Suraguru suravara pujita lingam, suravana pushpa sadarchita lingam |
Paratparam paramatmaka lingam, tat pranamami sada shiva lingam || 8 ||

Concluding Verse

Lingashtakam idam punyam yah pathet shiva-sannidhau |
Shiva-lokam avapnoti shivena saha modate ||
